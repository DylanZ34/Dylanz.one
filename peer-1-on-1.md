# Peer 1 on 1 - 4S Ranch

Peer 1 on 1 is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing social inclusion for youth on the autism spectrum and other developmental disorders. Our 4S Ranch branch brings the Peer 1 on 1 mission to the San Diego community.

## Our Mission

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to make friends, learn social skills, and feel included. Peer 1 on 1 creates fun, supportive environments where neurotypical and neurodiverse youth can interact, learn from each other, and build lasting friendships.

## What We Do

### Peer Clubs
Regular club meetings where youth can engage in fun activities while practicing social skills in a supportive environment.

### Social Events
We organize a variety of activities including:
- Movie nights
- Hiking adventures
- Sports days
- Game nights
- Art and craft sessions
- And more!

### Teen Volunteer Program
Teen volunteers (ages 9+) serve as role models and friends, earning community service hours while making a real difference in the lives of neurodiverse youth.

## Who We Serve

- **Youth ages 5-18** on the autism spectrum
- Youth with **developmental disorders** or **special needs**
- **Neurotypical peers** who want to build empathy and inclusion skills
- **Teen volunteers** looking to make a positive impact

## Get Involved

### For Families
Join our 4S Ranch community! Our programs provide your child with opportunities to:
- Make new friends
- Develop social skills
- Participate in fun activities
- Feel included and accepted

### For Teens
Become a peer volunteer! You'll:
- Earn community service hours
