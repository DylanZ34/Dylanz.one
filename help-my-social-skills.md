# Help My Social Skills

AI-guided tutorial for neurodivergent kids to develop social skills

## Welcome

Welcome to Help My Social Skills — an AI-powered guide designed specifically for neurodivergent children to navigate social interactions with confidence. Whether you're on the autism spectrum, have ADHD, or experience other neurodivergent traits, this program meets you where you are.

## What We Offer

### Personalized Learning Paths
Every person is unique. Our AI adapts to your learning style, pace, and interests to create a personalized social skills journey that works for you.

### Interactive Scenarios
Practice real-life social situations through engaging interactive scenarios:
- Starting a conversation with peers
- Understanding body language and facial expressions
- Taking turns in conversations
- Handling conflicts peacefully
- Making and keeping friends

### Emotion Recognition
Learn to identify and understand emotions — both yours and others'. Our guided exercises help build emotional intelligence through visual aids, practice exercises, and real-world applications.

### Safe Practice Environment
Make mistakes without judgment. Our supportive AI provides gentle feedback and celebrates progress, helping build confidence one step at a time.

## How It Works

1. **Assessment** — Start with a friendly assessment to understand your strengths and areas you want to improve
2. **Goal Setting** — Set personal social goals that matter to you
3. **Learn & Practice** — Work through interactive lessons and practice scenarios
4. **Feedback** — Get constructive feedback and celebrate wins
5. **Progress** — Track your growth and unlock new challenges

## Why It Works

- **Patient** — No rushing, go at your own pace
- **Non-judgmental** — Mistakes are learning opportunities
- **Flexible** — Learn anytime, anywhere
- **Supportive** — Encouragement at every step
- **Evidence-based** — Built on proven social skills research
